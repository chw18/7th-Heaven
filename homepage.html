7th Heaven DAY SPA

Welcome to 7th Heaven, a day spa that provides bliss, relaxation and rejuvenation for brides-to-be! Our packages are especially tailored for excited brides, engaged couples, and their circle of family and friends. We believe that weddings symbolize the meaningful commitment between two lovers, and therefore we specialize our services for this monumental celebration. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing, down-to-earth experience, or a fun, laughter-full time with your friends, we will provide you an extravagant experience not so far from your future paradise with your beloved.
